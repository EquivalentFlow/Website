Lorenzo Lorenzetti

I am a Postdoctoral Researcher at the Institute of Philosophy, Università della Svizzera italiana (USI), Lugano, Switzerland.

My research areas are philosophy of physics, philosophy of science, and metaphysics, particularly at their intersection. My main academic interest lies in inter-level relations in science (especially reduction in physics). I also work on foundational aspects of thermodynamics, the structure and ontology of spacetime theories, and the philosophy of quantum mechanics.

I did my PhD at the University of Bristol, supervised by James Ladyman and Karim Thébault. I have been a visiting researcher at the University of Pittsburgh and King’s College London, and a visiting graduate student at the University of Geneva.

I’m an editor for PhilPapers in the categories Interlevel Relations in Physical Science and Functionalism in Science.
