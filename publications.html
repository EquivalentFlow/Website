Publications

Peer-Reviewed Articles

  1. Lorenzetti, L. & Antoniou, A. Limiting Reduction and Modified Gravity. British Journal for the Philosophy of Science (forthcoming). Online since 2025 · Publisher · Preprint
    We argue that Modified Newtonian Dynamics (MOND) does not reduce adequately to Newtonian gravity, thereby failing a key condition for theoretical justification, and use this case to develop a refined account of limiting reduction.
  2. Lorenzetti, L. Making Sense of Gravitational Thermodynamics. Philosophy of Physics (2025). Publisher · Preprint
    I defend the applicability of equilibrium statistical mechanics and thermodynamics to long-range interacting systems—which exhibit phenomena like negative heat capacity and lack conventional equilibrium—in astrophysical contexts.
  3. Lorenzetti, L. Two Forms of Functional Reductionism in Physics. Synthese (2024). Publisher · Preprint
    A comprehensive guide to functional reduction as a model for reduction in physics. I review the Lewisian account and propose an alternative approach called Semantic Functional Reduction, taking the reduction of thermodynamic entropy as a case study.
  4. Lorenzetti, L. & Ladyman, J. Effective Ontic Structural Realism. British Journal for the Philosophy of Science (forthcoming). Online since 2023 · Publisher · Preprint
    We argue that Ontic Structural Realism can provide a reformulation and unification of different ‘effective realist’ proposals about scientific theories in terms of realism about modal structure and the idea of scale relativity of ontology.
  5. Lorenzetti, L. Functionalism, Reductionism, and Levels of Reality. Philosophy of Science (2023). Publisher · Preprint
    I raise a puzzle for the Lewisian approach to functional reduction, based on the tension between identity and asymmetry within the account. I then propose a solution that dissolves the tension.
  6. Lorenzetti, L. Functionalising the Wavefunction. Studies in History and Philosophy of Science (2022). Publisher · Preprint
    I present a functional reductionist account of classical systems to explain how macroscopic entities can be recovered from the quantum wavefunction.
  7. Lorenzetti, L. Structuralist Approaches to Bohmian Mechanics. Synthese (2022). Publisher · Preprint
    I discuss the application of structuralist metaphysics to the ontology of Bohmian mechanics, in particular Ontic Structural Realism and metaphysical coherentism.
  8. Lorenzetti, L. A Refined Propensity Account for GRW Theory. Foundations of Physics (2021). Publisher · Preprint
    I present a dispositionalist approach to GRW theory to account for the theory's objective probabilities and to provide an ontological account of the wave function.
  9. Lorenzetti, L. Humean Supervenience and Tripartite Entanglement Relations. Axiomathes (2020). Publisher · Preprint
    I discuss a tension between Humean Supervenience and quantum mechanics, arising from tripartite entanglement relations, such as GHZ states.
